# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to encourage participation in our project and ensure a welcoming and friendly experience for every contributor, where bias and discrimination are not tolerated. We value openness, creativity, honesty and hard work, and we try our best to ensure every voice is heard and respected. 

## Our Standards

Below are the accepted behaviors in communication and contribution:

- Maintaining an open mindset for various ideas raised by different people
- Remaining inclusive when hearing differnt voices, either praise or constructive criticism
- Showing courtesy towards other community members
- Using formal and polite language

The following behaviors should be avoided in our community:

- Use of offensive and/or aggressive languages
- Verbal, mental and/or physical attacks of other members
- Leaking other members' private information to the public, either for personal or business purposes
- Any other unprofessional and/or unrespectful approaches

## Enforcement

Project administrators reserve the rights to intervene in conflicts and protect everyone's granted rights, including but not limited to make necessary adjustments to the contents of the project repo, block or even remove someone from this community that do not abide by this code of conduct, etc.
